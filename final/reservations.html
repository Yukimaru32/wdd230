Rental Agreement & Information

A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations. A valid driver's license is required at the time of rental. No one under the age of 12 may ride on scooters. Fuel charges are not included. Enjoy unlimited mileage. Liability insurance is included, and damage insurance is available for purchase. Free delivery is available for rentals of 2+ days (to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs). We accept payment via Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo. Rentals may be returned to either of our locations.
